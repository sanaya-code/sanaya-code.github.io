ethod 1: Foreground Select Tool (Best for complex objects)
1. Open your image:
   - File > Open
   - Or drag image into GIMP

2. Prepare the layer:
   - Right-click layer(eye) in Layers panel. (On right hand side)
   - Select "Add Alpha Channel" (for transparency)

3. Select the Fuzzy Select Tool(looks like sesor):
   - Press [U] or find in toolbox
   - click on brackground
   - press delete to remove background
